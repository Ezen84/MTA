Second Half

This is the second half of the meat of the website, and i will be first starting off with Jamaica Depot.This is Jamaica Depot, one of the depots that I have close proximity to.They are currently(at the time of this fansite) reinnovating the depot so that they can store more buses and maybe accomadate for bus demands(thats good, because they really be crazy with these time stamps).

Jamaica Depot is home the Q3,Q4(my bus route-3 blocks off),Q5,Q17,Q30,Q84(also my bus route,)Q77,and Q85 bus routes.Most of them arent really that special, except for the Q5/Q85 and the Q3, which goes to the JFK airport.

This depot is well know for having the worst type of service(some may say Queens village is one of those depots that have the worst), but you cannot lie, this depot has alot going on, being the "Jack of all trades, master of none" type of deal. The routes for the Laguardia Depot are Q18, Q29, Q33, Q39, Q47, Q67, Q69, Q72, Q100, Q101, Q102, Q103, Q104, with them having two select bus serives and 3 express routes.

Queens Village Depot is one that I had never had much experience with, only rode the Q27 to Murdock AV sometimes, but that's about it. Queens Village Depot is home to the Q1,Q2,Q20A(shared with casey setengel depot),Q27,Q36,Q43,Q46,Q83, and Q88 routes, as well as the X63,X64,and X68 for express routes.
